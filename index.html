Enhancing Healthcare with Whole Patient Support

ANGeL is an AI-powered platform that supports healthcare providers in managing social care needs at scale.

Acting as an autonomous assistant, ANGeL conducts patient assessments, identifies social needs, resolves issues autonomously, escalates to human support as needed and executes closed-loop referrals, all while keeping providers informed.

ANGeL is rapidly deployable and exceptionally versatile, allowing Human Medical Professionals to focus on the most critical of issues.
